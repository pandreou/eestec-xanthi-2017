About the Event

Another very interesting training took place during the HOBoM, this time about presentation skills. Eight members of the local committee had the opportunity to attend the training and develop their skills both on presenting themselves and on presenting a topic they were asked to explicate. The training was carried out by the former EESTEC International president, Ashihan Bener from EESTEC LC Ankara and by our LC’s trainer, Maria Kouneli.
