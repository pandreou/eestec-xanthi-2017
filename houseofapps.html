Our Lab

We know the Chemistry and you know the business.

Welcome to our lab. This year, in LC Xanthi’s lab we are experimenting on the beer production. Our goal is to use automatic systems with the help of Arduino to perfect our BRewing and produce the ideal beer..

Obviously, in today's world, automatic systems are much needed in various scientific fields. They help reduce the cost of production while at the same time the final product becomes better and more effective. This is what we want to achieve in our lab with your help: use Arduinos to control the production of our beer and make sure the final draft is magical!

And now a few words about our city Xanthi. It is located in Northen Greece,specifically in Thrace. A beautiful city full of students and exciting places to visit, such asthe path of life, the old town with the traditional architecture and of course the little alleys with the beguiling smell of traditional cooking and drinks.
